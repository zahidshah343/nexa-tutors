Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our tutoring services

How do I book a free trial session?

You can book a free trial session by calling us, sending a WhatsApp message, or filling out the contact form above. We'll schedule a 30-minute assessment session at your convenience.

Do you offer online tutoring?

Yes, we offer both in-person and online tutoring options. Our online sessions are conducted via secure platforms with interactive whiteboards and screen sharing capabilities.

How quickly will you respond to my inquiry?

We respond to phone calls and WhatsApp messages immediately during business hours. Emails and contact form submissions are typically answered within 12-48 hours during business days.

Can I visit your center without an appointment?

While walk-ins are welcome, we recommend scheduling an appointment to ensure a team member is available to give you their full attention. You can call ahead or schedule through our website.
