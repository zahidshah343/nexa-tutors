Tutor Requirements

What we look for in our teaching team members

Essential Requirements

  • Bachelor's degree or higher in relevant subject (in progress or completed)
  • Strong academic record (minimum 3.0 GPA or equivalent)
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
  • Passion for teaching and student success
  • Availability for minimum 8 hours per week

Preferred Qualifications

  • Previous teaching/tutoring experience (1+ years preferred)
  • Master's degree or PhD in teaching subject
  • Experience with O/A Levels, Matric, or University curriculum
  • Familiarity with online teaching platforms and tools
  • Specialized expertise in advanced topics or exam preparation

Frequently Asked Questions

What qualifications do I need?

You should have expertise in your subject area with a bachelor's degree or higher. Teaching experience is preferred but not mandatory for fresh graduates with excellent academic records.

How much can I earn?

Earnings depend on your qualifications, experience, and availability. We offer competitive rates ranging from PKR 1,000-3,000 per hour based on your profile and subject expertise.

What support do tutors receive?

We provide teaching materials, curriculum guidance, training workshops, administrative support for scheduling and payments, and a collaborative community of educators.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about teaching with Nexa Tutors

What qualifications do I need to become a tutor?

Minimum requirement is a Bachelor's degree (or current student) in the relevant subject with strong academic performance. Teaching experience is preferred but not mandatory for exceptional candidates.

How are tutors compensated?

Tutors earn competitive hourly rates ranging from ₨ 800 to ₨ 2,000 per hour based on qualifications, experience, and subject demand. Performance bonuses and regular increments are also provided.

Can I teach online only?

Yes, we offer both in-person and online teaching options. You can choose your preferred mode. We provide training and support for online teaching platforms.

How flexible is the schedule?

Very flexible! You can choose your hours - morning, afternoon, evening, or weekends. Minimum commitment is 8 hours per week. You can increase or decrease hours based on availability.

What support do tutors receive?

We provide teaching materials, curriculum guidance, training workshops, administrative support for scheduling and payments, and a collaborative community of educators.
